Professional Experience

Anna is a freelance Full Stack Python Developer based in Harare, Zimbabwe who is currently employed as Django Girls Fundraising Coordinator; a position she has held since June 2017. She is a holder of a BSc (Hons.) in Computer Science from the Midlands State University and an MSc in Strategic Management from Chinhoyi University. Anna has vast experience in various ICT disciplines having worked in various positions in the ICT industry in Zimbabwe and globally.

Leadership & Community Experience

Anna is currently the President of the Django Software Foundation (DSF) Board of Directors 2021. She has been a member of the DSF Board of Directors since 2018 and was the Vice President of the DSF from 2019 to 2020. She is also a Trustee of the Django Girls Foundation and Python Zimbabwe Trust. She also volunteers as Fundraising Coordinator for Django Girls Foundation. She also volunteered for the DSF Code of Conduct Committee in 2017.

Anna has also organised many events in different parts of the world. These include PyCon Zimbabwe (2016 & 2017), several Django Girls Harare events, Django Girls Masvingo, Django Girls Heidelberg, and DjangoCon Europe 2018. She is currently working on introducing a DjangoCon on the African continent.

She has spoken at many Python/Django events across the globe. Anna has given keynote speeches at PyCon Namibia 2017, DjangoCon Europe 2017, DjangoCon US 2018, and PyCon Africa 2019. Other events she has spoken at include PyCon Zimbabwe 2016, PyCon Italy 2017, Codemotion Milan 2017, PyCon Namibia 2016 - 2018, and PyCon South Africa 2016.

Anna likes to mentor other ladies who are interested in learning code. She organised and ran PyLadies Harare from 2016 -2018.
