Volunteer Work

Anna is a member of PyZim, a local group of Python Programmers in Zimbabwe. Her work with PyZim has seen her working together with Humphrey Butau to organise the first ever Django Girls Harare to be held in Harare on 9th April 2016. Django Girls Harare is a non-profit affiliation of Django Girls international which aims to introduce women in Zimbabwe to the programming industry by mentoring and coaching them in Python/Django web development.


Anna's involvement with Django Girls Harare enabled her to be a part of PyCon Namibia 2016 held 25th - 29th January 2016. PyCon Namibia offered Anna the chance to coach at Django Girls Windhoek and get a peek of what it would be like to organise, host and coach at a Django Girls Workshop. Anna also gave a talk on About Learning to Program and also conducted a workshop on Using PyThon/Django to Solve Real Life Problems. All these activities were mainly for beginners and aimed at helping attendees who were new to programming find their way in their programming field.
