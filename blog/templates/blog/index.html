About me


Welcome to Anna Makarudze's personal website, which gives you more information about ME!

Anna Makarudze is young woman aged 29, born on the 30th June 1986 in Chibi, Masvingo, Zimbabwe. Anna is an entreprenuer, ICT consultant, Python/Django software developer, researcher and a Django Girls organiser. She is currently working on starting up and establishing her own software development and consultancy firm, Anntele Business Solutions P/L. Anna hopes to promote and advance the use of open source software in Africa through using open source programs in software development. She also hopes to empower other young people, especially women through Django Girls workshops and other open source meetups.

Educational Profile

Anna Makarudze is a holder of a Master of Science in Strategic Management from Chinhoyi University of Technology, a Bachelor of Science (Honours) Degree in Computer Science from Midlands State University (2009) and a Certificate in Customer Relationship Management from the University of South Africa.
