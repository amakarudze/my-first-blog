Educational Profile

Anna started out her Computer Science and programming learning at Zimuto High School in Masvingo where she studied GCE Ordinary Level Computer Studies (2002)and GCE Advanced Level Computing (2004). She pursued her computer studies through university at Midlands State University, Gweru, Zimbabwe where she studied for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.


Anna Makarudze is a holder of a Master of Science in Strategic Management from Chinhoyi University of Technology (2015), a Bachelor of Science (Honours) Degree in Computer Science from Midlands State University (2009) and a Certificate in Customer Relationship Management from the University of South Africa. Anna has plans to be starting her Phd studies soon by doing an MPhil/DPhil in Computer Science and Informatics specialising in machine learning and data mining


Anna has done several professional courses related to ICT, which include:

  • Learn Python, Treehouse & Muzinda Hub, Zimbabwe – 2015
  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) Discovery, St Georges College, Zimbabwe – 2010
  • 6419A: Configuring, Managing, Maintaining Windows Server® 2008 Servers, ICT Academy, Zimbabwe – 2010
  • 6425B: Configuring and Troubleshooting Windows Server® 2008 Active Directory® Domain Services, ICT Academy, Zimbabwe – 2010
